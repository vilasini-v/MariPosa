About Company


Mariposa, a platform connecting nurseries and buyers, champions sustainability through its SDG-aligned mission. All revenue empowers NGOs fighting hunger and deforestation, while detailed plant guides promote responsible practices. This win-win fosters a thriving community where nurseries gain wider reach, buyers find ethical plants, and the environment flourishes. Join Mariposa and cultivate a greener, more equitable future, one plant-at-a-time.

What Makes Us Different?


Following points below makes us unique

  1. Option to purchase Online or Offline
  2. Detailed steps on how to maintain the plant: For eg.when to water it, change soil, what type of nutrients and soil are to be used etc.
  3. Part of the proceeds go towards charities that battle to end hunger.
